Viva Vigan City!

Welcome to Vigan, a city steeped in history, culture, and architectural marvels that will transport you to another era. Nestled in the heart of the Philippines,
this charming destination is a must-visit for travelers seeking a unique and enchanting experience. Whether you are a history buff, a culture vulture,
or simply someone in search of awe-inspiring sights, Vigan has something for everyone.

Baluarte Zoo

The Baluarte is one of the must-visit places in Vigan. It is owned by Ilocos former governor and prominent political figure, Luis Chavit Singson. The 80-or-more-hectare Vigan tourist spot gained popularity for having a mini zoo, safari gallery, chapel, and resort that can easily suit what a tourist needs.

THE BEAUTY OF VIGAN

Vigans appeal is insanely timeless, if it is bristling with superb choices of places to explore. This vibrant city is the capital of Ilocos Sur and one of few surviving old historic cities in the Philippines. When visiting Vigan, it is highly recommended that you stay there overnight to be able to explore most of its beautiful and nostalgic spots. Be dazzled by luxuriousness of this sentimental city bursting with a tons of Kalesa (horse-drawn carriage) and ancestral houses inspired by Spanish, Mexican and Chinese architecture.

APAY NGA VIGAN TI
PILYEM KAS!

The heritage city of Vigan in Ilocos Sur is still in the running to become one of the 7 Wonder Cities of the World. In this penultimate round of the race, this northern city made it to the list of 21 finalists and will compete against the worlds best cities such as Bangkok, Barcelona, Istanbul, London, Mexico City, Mumbai, Perth, Reykjavik, Saint Petersburg, and Seoul to make it to the next round.
