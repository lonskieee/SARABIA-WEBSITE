Festivities and Events in
Vigan City!

The celebration of the Viva Vigan Festival is a testament to the Philippines’ cultural diversity and rich traditions. It stands as a proud beacon highlighting the Filipino’s capability to protect and celebrate its heritage in the face of modernity. The festival’s events, from street parades adorned with intricately designed carrozas (carriages) to competition of calesas (horse-drawn carriages), and the showcase of indigenous cuisines, embody the communal spirit and artistic flair of the Filipino people.
